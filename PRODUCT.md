# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who want to make their life easier working with coding agents, including while away from their desk. The primary audience is broad but technical enough to run agents such as Codex, Pi, and OpenCode, then manage them from desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose
Sidemesh is an AI agent orchestrator for coding-agent sessions. It exists to let people run and manage coding agents from both desktop and mobile, keep control of sessions while on the go, and make agent-driven workflows easier to monitor and operate without adding unnecessary complexity.

## Brand Personality
Simple, clear, capable. The tone should feel approachable and easy to understand, while still conveying that the product is powerful and trustworthy for real work.

## Anti-references
Do not make this feel like crypto. Do not make it feel like enterprise bloat. Do not drift into hacker-neon aesthetics. Avoid UI patterns that feel intimidating, over-decorated, or overly insider-focused when a simpler and more understandable solution exists.

## Design Principles
1. Make powerful workflows feel understandable.
2. Preserve control while reducing friction.
3. Design for real use away from the desk, not just ideal desktop setups.
4. Prefer clarity over technical theatrics.
5. Keep the interface broadly legible to most users who are curious about coding agents, not just experts steeped in tooling culture.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard accessibility good practice applies by default. Prioritize readable hierarchy, clear labels, sensible contrast, straightforward interaction patterns, and a UI that remains understandable without relying on insider jargon or decorative effects.
